## Description

What if you could hear from 16 different personalities before making your next big decision?

16TypeTalk creates a personal council of MBTI personalities that discuss your real problems, decisions, and dilemmas — from careers to relationships to life choices.

Choose your council. Share your topic. Watch 16 unique voices debate, challenge, and illuminate your situation from angles you never considered.

HOW IT WORKS:
→ Describe your situation or decision
→ Select 2-5 MBTI personalities as your council
→ Each personality responds with their authentic perspective
→ Vote on which voice resonates most
→ Receive a personalized insight report

YOUR COUNCIL INCLUDES:
⚡ ENTJ — The Commander: Direct, strategic, action-oriented
🌊 INFP — The Mediator: Empathetic, value-driven, introspective
🔭 INTJ — The Architect: Analytical, systematic, visionary
💡 ENTP — The Debater: Creative, challenging, innovative
🌙 INFJ — The Advocate: Intuitive, empathetic, principled
...and 11 more unique perspectives

FEATURES:
• AI-powered MBTI personality dialogues
• Personalized insight reports after each session
• Vote on perspectives that resonate with you
• Build custom councils or use Quick Council
• 6 languages: English, Turkish, Korean, Japanese, Portuguese, Spanish
• Beautiful dark-mode interface
• Privacy-first: conversations processed in real-time, not stored

PRIVACY FIRST:
Your conversations are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers. Your thoughts stay yours.
